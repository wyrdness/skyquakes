## Source Notes

This bibliography encompasses peer-reviewed scientific publications, government technical reports, books, media coverage, and online resources related to skyquake phenomena. Sources span multiple disciplines including seismology, atmospheric physics, oceanography, and acoustic science.

Historical sources (particularly pre-1950) may be available only in specialized libraries or archives. Many government reports are accessible through agency websites or document repositories.

For current research and events:
- Monitor USGS Earthquake Hazards Program for seismic activity
- Check CTBTO infrasound network data for atmospheric detections
- Follow scientific journals in seismology and atmospheric science
- Engage with citizen science platforms for real-time reports
